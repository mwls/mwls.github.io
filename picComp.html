Picture of the Night Sky - Competition

Night Sky - Artistic Photo Competition!

As part of the coursework for the Introduction to Astrophysics course, the students have had to take a photo of the night sky (or anything in the night sky). Each year we give out some prizes in certain catagories. This year the catagories are:

  • Best Photo
  • Best Artistic Photo
  • Best Phone Photo
  • Best Deep Sky Object
  • Best Moon

The two catagories in bold are our 'main' catagories, and all the shortlisted images included below are eligilbe for these prizes. The images have been split into whichever 'minor' catagories they are also eligible for. Click on any photo to see the full image and extra information about equipment and location. There's a bit of a gap between sections, so make sure you reach the bottom of the page!

To decide the winners I'm gathering the rankings of anyone in the department; there's two options:

  • Short Option: Please send your ranking of your favourite images (approx top 5 would be great).
  • Long Option: If your willing to spend a few minutes, please send your rankings for each of the catagories listed above.

Thanks everyone for your help, this year I've promised to announce the winners!
