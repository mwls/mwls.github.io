Picture of the Night Sky - Competition

Night Sky - Artistic Photo Competition!

As part of the coursework for the Introduction to Astrophysics course, the students have had to take a photo of the night sky (or anything in the night sky). Each year we give out some prizes in certain catagories. This year the catagories are:

  • Best Photo
  • Best Moon
  • Best Deep Sky Milky Way
  • Best Extragalactic
  • Best Phone Photo
  • Best Landscape Photo

The 'Best Photo' catagory (in bold) is our 'main' prize, and all the shortlisted images included below are eligilbe for this prize. The images have been split into whichever 'minor' catagories they are also eligible for. Click on any photo to see the full image and extra information about equipment and location. There's a bit of a gap between sections, so make sure you reach the bottom of the page!

To decide the winners I'm gathering the rankings of anyone in the department; there's two options:

  • Short Option: Please send your ranking of your favourite images (approx top 5 would be great) across all the catagories.
  • Long Option: If you're willing to spend a few minutes, please send your rankings for each of the catagories listed above, as well as your rankings for best photo (5 great, but more or less fine).

Votes should be e-mailed to me, and I'll collate everyone's responses. The winner will be announced at the revision lecture on Friday - thanks for your help!
