Matthew Smith's Personal Webpage

Publications

During my research career I have worked on studies on many different aspects of galaxy evolution, mainly concentrating on dust and gas in galaxies. I've been fortunate that I've had significant roles in a few large surveys, for example being the data lead for H-ATLAS or JINGLE and so have worked on many publications. Below I give some basic stats and a list of all the publications I have authored below. All the publication information is taken from my Google Scholar, Cardiff ORCA and NASA ADS.
