Publications

During my research career I have worked on studies on many different aspects of galaxy evolution, mainly concentrating on dust and gas in galaxies. I've been fortunate that I've had significant roles in a few large surveys, for example being the data lead for H-ATLAS or JINGLE and so have worked on many publications. Below I give some basic stats and a list of all the publications I have authored below. All the publication information is taken from my ADS library (Here). You can also find information on these publications on Google Scholar and Cardiff ORCA.

My PhD thesis "The Herschel Perspective on Galaxy Evolution" completed in 2013 is available here.
